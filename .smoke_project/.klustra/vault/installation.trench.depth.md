# Installation Trench Depth

The trench depth for direct burial of high-voltage cables depends on the location. In urban areas, the minimum depth is 800 mm; in rural areas, the minimum depth is 1200 mm ^[8913d160a82c281a:Direct Burial]. These depths provide sufficient cover to protect the cable from mechanical damage and are typically specified in national standards and [[local.electrical.safety.regulations]].

## Related installation steps

- The trench is excavated to the required depth as part of [[installation.direct.burial]].
- A [[installation.sand.bedding]] layer of 100 mm is placed below and above the cable.
- [[installation.warning.tape]] is placed 300 mm above the cable to alert future excavators.

## Compliance and guidance

Installers must always verify local requirements, as some regions may mandate greater depths depending on soil conditions or traffic loads. The standard depths given here serve as minimum values.
