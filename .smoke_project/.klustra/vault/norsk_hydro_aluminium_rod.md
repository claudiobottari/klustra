Norsk Hydro supplies aluminium rod used as a conductor material in high-voltage cable systems. The rod is certified to IEC 60228, which specifies the requirements for conductors of insulated cables. This certification ensures that the aluminium rod meets the necessary electrical and mechanical standards for use in HV cable applications. Norsk Hydro is listed on the approved supplier list for HV cable components, specifically for conductor materials.^[66fee6e56f742178:1-3]
