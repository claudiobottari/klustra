## Descrizione generale

High-voltage (HV) cable systems are critical infrastructure for power transmission. Modern HV cables use cross-linked polyethylene (XLPE) insulation for voltages up to 500 kV.^[8085135b0a96974f:2]

## Componenti principali

The cable core consists of a [[copper.conductor]] or [[aluminium.conductor]] surrounded by:
- [[semi-conductive.screen]]
- [[xlpe.insulation]] layer
- [[metallic.sheath]] for water blocking
- [[outer.protective.jacket]]^[8085135b0a96974f:4–6]

## Applicazioni

HV cables are deployed in [[underground.urban.networks]], [[submarine.crossings]], and connections to [[offshore.wind.farm.connections]]. Installation requires careful handling of [[bending.radii]] and thermal considerations.^[8085135b0a96974f:8–9]
