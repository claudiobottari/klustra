**Borealis Visico LE4253** is a [[peroxide.crosslinking|peroxide-crosslinkable]] [[xlpe.insulation|XLPE compound]] used as the primary insulation in [[high-voltage.cable.systems|high-voltage power cables]]. It is listed on the approved supplier list for HV cable components, indicating its suitability for demanding applications such as [[underground.urban.networks]], [[submarine.crossings]], and [[offshore.wind.farm.connections]].^[66fee6e56f742178]

The compound is designed to be processed via the [[triple.extrusion.process]] and is classified as a [[super.clean.xlpe.compound|super-clean XLPE compound]] to minimize contaminants that could lead to [[water.treeing]] or [[electrical.treeing]]. It includes an [[antioxidant.package]] to enhance long-term thermal stability. Borealis Visico LE4253 is typically used with [[copper.conductor|copper]] or [[aluminium.conductor|aluminium conductors]] and is compatible with standard [[semi-conductive.screen|semi-conductive screens]] and [[metallic.sheath|metallic sheaths]].^[66fee6e56f742178]

As an approved material, it meets the requirements of relevant standards such as [[iec.62067]] and is subject to [[cable.testing.type_testing|type testing]] and [[cable.testing.routine_testing|routine testing]] to ensure insulation integrity. The compound's properties influence key installation parameters, including [[bending.radii|minimum bending radii]] and [[installation.maximum.pulling.tension|maximum pulling tension]].^[66fee6e56f742178]
