## Regulatory framework

All HV cable work must comply with [[iec.61936-1]], [[local.electrical.safety.regulations]], and **company-specific safety procedures**^[46d617e505500254:1]. These procedures are defined by the operating company and may cover additional requirements for isolation, earthing, voltage verification, locking/tagging, and safe work zones beyond those in the base standards.

## Relationship to other standards

Company-specific safety procedures are not a substitute for national or international regulations; they are an additional layer of control. They are typically documented in the company’s safety management system and are mandatory for all personnel involved in HV cable installation, maintenance, or fault response.
