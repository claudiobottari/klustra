## Overview

**trench_depth** refers to the minimum vertical distance from the ground surface to the top of a cable trench, a critical parameter in the direct burial of high-voltage cable systems. The required depth is determined by cable type, soil conditions, and local regulations to protect the cable from mechanical damage, thermal effects, and environmental factors. Two common standards exist:
- **Urban areas**: minimum trench depth of 800 mm from the ground surface to the cable top.
- **Rural areas**: minimum trench depth of 1200 mm from the ground surface to the cable top.

These depths are part of a broader direct burial installation procedure that includes sand bedding, warning tape, and backfill. ^[4548a58ab1ab402c:14-17]

## Installation Requirements

For direct burial, the cable must be placed in a trench excavated to the specified [[trench_depth]]. The trench bottom is first prepared with a 100 mm layer of sand [[sand_bedding]] below the cable. After cable placement, another 100 mm sand bedding layer is added above the cable, followed by a [[warning_tape]] positioned 300 mm above the cable. The trench is then backfilled with compacted excavated material ([[backfill]]). ^[4548a58ab1ab402c:14-17]

These minimum depths (800 mm urban, 1200 mm rural) are general guidelines and may be superseded by local electrical safety regulations or company-specific procedures (see [[iec_61936_1]], [[local_electrical_safety_regulations]], [[company_specific_safety_procedures]]).

## Safety and Compliance

Proper trench depth ensures that the cable is below the zone of typical surface activities (e.g., shallow excavation, landscaping, light vehicular traffic). Deeper burial in rural areas accounts for potential agricultural machinery loads and deeper frost penetration. Compliance with the minimum trench depth is verified during [[after_installation_testing]] procedures such as [[sheath_integrity_test]]. ^[4548a58ab1ab402c:14-17]

## Related Parameters

The trench depth interacts with other installation parameters:
- Minimum bending radius (20× cable outer diameter) ^[4548a58ab1ab402c:5]
- Maximum pulling tension (50 N/mm² on conductor) ^[4548a58ab1ab402c:6]
- Installation temperature (>0 °C) ^[4548a58ab1ab402c:7]
- Joint bay dimensions (3 m × 1.5 m) for [[jointing]] operations ^[4548a58ab1ab402c:19-20]
