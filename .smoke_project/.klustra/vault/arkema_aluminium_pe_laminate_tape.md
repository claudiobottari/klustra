**Arkema aluminium-PE laminate tape** is a sheathing component used in the manufacture of high-voltage power cables. It consists of a thin aluminium foil laminated to a polyethylene (PE) layer, providing a **radial moisture barrier** that protects the cable core (especially [[xlpe_insulation]]) from water ingress. The tape is supplied by Arkema, a chemicals and advanced materials company, and is listed among approved suppliers for [[hv_cable_components]] sheathing materials.^[66fee6e56f742178:0]

In practice, the aluminium-PE laminate tape is applied as a longitudinal or helical wrap around the cable core, often combined with an outer HDPE sheath. The aluminium layer acts as a hermetic seal, while the PE layer ensures compatibility with the surrounding polymeric insulation and facilitates bonding during extrusion. This system is critical for maintaining the long-term reliability of [[high_voltage_cable_systems]] in wet or buried environments.^[66fee6e56f742178:0]

Arkema is listed alongside ExxonMobil (which supplies an HDPE compound for the same purpose) in the [[approved_supplier_list_hv_cable_components]], indicating that the tape meets the required quality and performance standards for use in [[hv_cable_core_components]] and [[hv_cable_direct_burial_installation]].^[66fee6e56f742178:0]
