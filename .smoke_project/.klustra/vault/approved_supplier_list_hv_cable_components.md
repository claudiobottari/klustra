The **approved supplier list for HV cable components** is a curated register of vendors whose materials and products have been qualified for use in [[high_voltage_cable_systems]]. The list covers four main categories: conductors, insulation compounds, sheathing materials, and accessories.

## Conductors
- **Norsk Hydro** – supplies aluminium rod certified to IEC 60228.^[66fee6e56f742178:1]
- **Aurubis AG** – supplies oxygen-free copper rod certified to EN 13601.^[66fee6e56f742178:1]

## Insulation Compounds
- **Borealis** – supplies Visico LE4253, a peroxide-crosslinkable [[xlpe_insulation]] compound.^[66fee6e56f742178:2]
- **Dow Chemical** – supplies HFDA-4202 EC, an extra-clean XLPE compound.^[66fee6e56f742178:2]

## Sheathing
- **ExxonMobil** – supplies HDPE compound used for radial moisture barriers.^[66fee6e56f742178:3]
- **Arkema** – supplies aluminium-PE laminate tape.^[66fee6e56f742178:3]

## Accessories
- **Prysmian** – supplies pre-moulded joints and terminations that are IEC type-tested.^[66fee6e56f742178:4]
- **NKT** – supplies cold-shrink joints for the 66–170 kV voltage range.^[66fee6e56f742178:4]

All listed suppliers and products are subject to the quality and testing requirements defined in the [[hv_cable_regulatory_framework]] and relevant standards such as [[iec_62067]].
