The minimum bending radius is a critical parameter during [[hv_cable_installation_bending_radius|high voltage cable installation]] to avoid excessive mechanical stress on the [[cable_core_components]] and [[xlpe_insulation]].

## General requirement
During installation, the minimum bending radius must be 20 times the cable outer diameter.^[8913d160a82c281a:1] This applies to both [[hv_cable_duct_installation_process|duct installation]] and [[hv_cable_direct_burial_installation|direct burial]] methods.

## Related installation parameters
- [[hv_cable_installation_pulling_tension]]: maximum 50 N/mm² on the conductor^[8913d160a82c281a:1]
- [[hv_cable_installation_temperature_conditions]]: installation temperature must be above 0 °C^[8913d160a82c281a:1]
