## Approved Supplier List – HV Cable Components

This page documents the list of approved suppliers for high-voltage cable components, as specified by the project's procurement guidelines. All suppliers listed below have been qualified through technical evaluation and certification to relevant international standards.^[66fee6e56f742178:narrative]

### Conductor
- **Aluminium rod**: [[norsk.hydro.aluminium.rod|Norsk Hydro]] – certified to IEC 60228.^[66fee6e56f742178:narrative]
- **Copper rod**: [[aurubis.ag.copper.rod|Aurubis AG]] – oxygen‑free copper, certified to EN 13601.^[66fee6e56f742178:narrative]

### Insulation Compound
- **XLPE compound**: [[borealis.visico.le4253.xlpe.compound|Borealis Visico LE4253]] – peroxide‑crosslinkable XLPE.^[66fee6e56f742178:narrative]
- **Extra‑clean XLPE compound**: [[dow.chemical.hfda-4202.ec.xlpe.compound|Dow Chemical HFDA‑4202 EC]].^[66fee6e56f742178:narrative]

### Sheathing & Moisture Barrier
- **HDPE moisture‑barrier compound**: [[exxonmobil.hdpe.compound.moisture.barrier|ExxonMobil HDPE compound]].^[66fee6e56f742178:narrative]
- **Aluminium‑PE laminate tape**: [[arkema.aluminium.pe.laminate.tape|Arkema Al‑PE laminate tape]].^[66fee6e56f742178:narrative]

### Accessories (Joints & Terminations)
- **Pre‑moulded joints and terminations**: [[prysmian.pre-moulded.joints.terminations|Prysmian]] – IEC type‑tested (see [[iec.62067]]).^[66fee6e56f742178:narrative]
- **Cold‑shrink joints (66–170 kV)**: [[nkt.cold-shrink.joints.66-170.kv|NKT]].^[66fee6e56f742178:narrative]
