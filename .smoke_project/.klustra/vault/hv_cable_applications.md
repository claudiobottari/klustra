# hv_cable_applications

High-voltage (HV) cable systems are deployed in a variety of critical infrastructure applications. Modern HV cables, typically using [[xlpe_insulation]], are used for power transmission at voltages up to 500 kV^[8085135b0a96974f:High-Voltage Cable Systems].

## Common applications

HV cables are installed in:
- Underground urban networks, where they replace overhead lines for aesthetic and safety reasons.
- Submarine crossings, connecting islands or crossing bodies of water.
- Connections to offshore wind farms, transmitting power from sea-based turbines to onshore grids.

These applications require careful handling of [[hv_cable_installation_bending_radius|bending radii]] and [[hv_cable_installation_temperature_conditions|thermal considerations]] during installation^[8085135b0a96974f:High-Voltage Cable Systems].
