## Overview

Prysmian pre‑moulded joints and terminations are a product line of [[high_voltage_cable_systems|high‑voltage cable accessories]] manufactured by Prysmian. They are supplied as factory‑assembled units that are ready for field installation, reducing the risk of installation errors and ensuring consistent electrical performance. The accessories are IEC type‑tested, meaning they have passed a series of standardised electrical, thermal and mechanical tests defined by the International Electrotechnical Commission.^[f55e45b580b53352:1]

## Technical characteristics

- **Construction**: Pre‑moulded silicone or EPDM rubber bodies that provide the required [[dielectric_strength|dielectric strength]] and [[partial_discharge_measurement|partial‑discharge]] performance. The design includes integrated stress‑control elements and a conductive outer layer for earthing.
- **Voltage range**: While the source does not specify a voltage class, pre‑moulded accessories of this type are typically used for systems from 66 kV up to 170 kV, and are compatible with [[cross_linked_polyethylene_insulation|XLPE‑insulated cables]].
- **Testing**: The IEC type‑test programme covers [[high_voltage_withstand_test|high‑voltage withstand]], [[partial_discharge_measurement|partial‑discharge measurement]], thermal cycling, and mechanical robustness. Routine tests on each unit include a [[conductor_resistance_check|conductor resistance check]] and a [[sheath_integrity_test|sheath integrity test]].^[f55e45b580b53352:1]

## Applications

Prysmian pre‑moulded joints and terminations are used in [[underground_urban_networks|underground urban networks]], [[submarine_crossings|submarine crossings]], and [[offshore_wind_farms|offshore wind farms]]. They are designed for direct burial, duct installation, or installation in [[joint_bay_dimensions|joint bays]] and termination structures.

## Installation considerations

- The accessories must be installed in accordance with the manufacturer’s instructions and [[local_electrical_safety_regulations|local electrical safety regulations]].
- Prior to installation, the cable must be isolated and earthed ([[cable_isolation_and_earthing|cable isolation and earthing]]), and [[voltage_absence_verification|voltage absence verification]] performed.
- The [[minimum_bending_radius|minimum bending radius]] and [[maximum_pulling_tension|maximum pulling tension]] of the cable must be respected during handling.
- After installation, [[after_installation_testing|after‑installation testing]] such as [[dc_vlf_withstand_testing|DC or VLF withstand testing]] and [[joint_resistance_measurement|joint resistance measurement]] is recommended to verify the integrity of the assembly.
