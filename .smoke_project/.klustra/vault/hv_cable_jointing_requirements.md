## General Requirements

All joints must be installed in a controlled environment to prevent contamination and ensure quality.^[8913d160a82c281a] Prefabricated joints are preferred for faster installation compared to field‑assembled types.^[8913d160a82c281a]

## Joint Bay Specifications

Joint bays must have minimum dimensions of 3 m × 1.5 m to provide adequate working space.^[8913d160a82c281a]
