## Overview

**ExxonMobil HDPE sheathing** is a high-density polyethylene (HDPE) compound approved for use as a radial moisture barrier in high-voltage cable systems. It is listed on the approved supplier list for sheathing components in [[hv_cable_systems]].^[66fee6e56f742178:1]

## Application

The compound is specifically designated for the outer sheathing layer of [[high_voltage_cable_systems]], where it provides a robust radial moisture barrier. This function is critical to protect the [[xlpe_insulation]] and other [[cable_core_components]] from water ingress, which can lead to premature failure.^[66fee6e56f742178:1]

## Approval status

ExxonMobil HDPE sheathing is included in the approved supplier list for HV cable components, alongside other sheathing materials such as [[arkema_aluminium_pe_laminate_tape]].^[66fee6e56f742178:1]
