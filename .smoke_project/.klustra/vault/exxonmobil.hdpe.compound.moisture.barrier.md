## Overview

**ExxonMobil HDPE compound** is a high-density polyethylene material approved for use as a radial moisture barrier in high-voltage cable systems. It is included in the approved supplier list for HV cable components, specifically under the sheathing category.^[66fee6e56f742178:0]

## Application

This compound is used to form a moisture barrier layer in cables, protecting the insulation (typically [[xlpe.insulation]]) and conductor ([[copper.conductor]] or [[aluminium.conductor]]) from water ingress. It is part of the sheathing system that may also include an [[arkema.aluminium.pe.laminate.tape]] for additional moisture protection.^[66fee6e56f742178:0]

## Approval Status

ExxonMobil HDPE compound is listed on the approved supplier list for HV cable components, alongside other sheathing materials such as Arkema's aluminium-PE laminate tape.^[66fee6e56f742178:0]
