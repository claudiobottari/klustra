**Prysmian pre-moulded joints and terminations** are factory-moulded, field-installable accessories for [[high_voltage_cable_systems]]. They are designed for use with [[xlpe_insulation]] cables and are type-tested according to [[iec_62067]].

## Design and construction

Prysmian pre-moulded joints and terminations are manufactured from silicone rubber or EPDM, with a conductive or semi-conductive stress-control layer integrated into the moulding. The pre-moulded body is expanded and shipped on a removable support core; during installation the core is extracted, allowing the accessory to shrink onto the prepared cable. This design eliminates the need for taping or heat-shrink components at the joint or termination position.

## Type testing and compliance

The accessories are IEC type-tested, as specified in the approved supplier list for [[hv_cable_jointing_requirements]].^[66fee6e56f742178:0] The type-test programme includes thermal cycling, partial discharge measurement, high-voltage withstand tests, and sheath integrity tests in accordance with [[iec_62067]].

## Application range

Prysmian pre-moulded joints and terminations are approved for use on XLPE-insulated cables in transmission and distribution networks. They are listed alongside [[nkt_cold_shrink_joints]] in the approved supplier list for [[accessory_suppliers_hv_cables]].^[66fee6e56f742178:0]

## Installation requirements

Installation must follow the manufacturer’s instructions, which specify minimum [[hv_cable_installation_bending_radius]], maximum [[hv_cable_installation_pulling_tension]], and ambient [[hv_cable_installation_temperature_conditions]]. The jointing process is covered under [[hv_cable_jointing_requirements]] and must comply with [[hv_cable_safe_working_practices]] and [[hv_cable_emergency_procedures]].

## Quality assurance

As an approved accessory, Prysmian pre-moulded joints and terminations are subject to [[hv_cable_routine_testing]] and [[hv_cable_after_installation_testing]], including [[partial_discharge_measurement]], [[high_voltage_withstand_test]], [[sheath_integrity_test]], and [[joint_resistance_measurement]].
