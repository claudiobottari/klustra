## Regulatory Framework

All [[hv_cable_safety_standards|HV cable work]] must comply with:
- IEC 61936-1 (Power installations exceeding 1 kV)
- Local electrical safety regulations
- Company-specific safety procedures^[46d617e505500254:1]

## Safe Working Practices

Before any work on cable systems:
1. Isolate and earth the cable at both ends
2. Verify absence of voltage using approved indicators
3. Apply safety locks and tags
4. Establish safe work zone with barriers^[46d617e505500254:2]

## Emergency Procedures

In case of cable fault during live operation:
- Maintain exclusion zone of 3 m around suspected fault location
- Do not attempt to re-energize without inspection
- Report immediately to control centre^[46d617e505500254:3]
