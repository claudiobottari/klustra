# XLPE Insulation Material

Cross-linked polyethylene (XLPE) is the dominant insulation material for
high-voltage cable systems operating at 66 kV and above.

## Properties

- Dielectric strength: 20-30 kV/mm
- Operating temperature: up to 90°C continuous
- Emergency rating: 130°C for short duration
- Low dielectric loss (tan δ < 0.001)

## Manufacturing Process

XLPE insulation is produced by peroxide crosslinking during extrusion.
The triple-extrusion process applies conductor screen, insulation, and
insulation screen in a single pass to eliminate interfaces.

## Degradation Mechanisms

Water treeing and electrical treeing are the primary aging mechanisms.
Modern super-clean XLPE compounds with antioxidant packages achieve
design lifetimes exceeding 40 years.
