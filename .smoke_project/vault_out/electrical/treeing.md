## Electrical Treeing

**Electrical treeing** is a primary aging mechanism in [[xlpe.insulation]] for [[high-voltage.cable.systems]], alongside [[water.treeing]]. It is a degradation process that can lead to insulation failure over time. Modern [[super.clean.xlpe.compound]]s with [[antioxidant.package]]s are designed to mitigate this mechanism, achieving design lifetimes exceeding 40 years.^[392adbd3c4ad7a29:XLPE Insulation Material]
