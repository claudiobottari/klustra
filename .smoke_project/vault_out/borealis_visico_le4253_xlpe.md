## Overview

**Borealis Visico LE4253** is a peroxide-crosslinkable [[xlpe_insulation|XLPE]] compound approved for use as an insulation material in [[high_voltage_cable_systems|high-voltage cable systems]]. It is listed on the approved supplier list for insulation compounds for [[hv_cable_applications|HV cable applications]].^[66fee6e56f742178:1]

## Composition and Properties

Visico LE4253 is a peroxide-crosslinkable XLPE compound.^[66fee6e56f742178:1] It is designed to meet the stringent cleanliness and performance requirements for [[xlpe_insulation_properties|XLPE insulation]] in high-voltage cables. The compound is manufactured by Borealis and is one of two approved insulation compounds, the other being Dow HFDA-4202 EC.^[66fee6e56f742178:1]

## Quality and Approval

The compound is included in the approved supplier list for HV cable components, specifically under the insulation compound category.^[66fee6e56f742178:1] This listing indicates that it has been evaluated and accepted for use in [[xlpe_compound_quality|XLPE compound quality]] assurance processes for [[hv_cable_safety_standards|HV cable safety standards]] and [[hv_cable_regulatory_framework|regulatory frameworks]].

## Applications

Visico LE4253 is used in the manufacture of [[cable_core_components|cable core components]] for high-voltage cables, where [[xlpe_insulation|XLPE insulation]] is required. It is suitable for [[hv_cable_applications|HV cable applications]] including [[hv_cable_direct_burial_installation|direct burial]] and [[hv_cable_duct_installation_process|duct installation]].
