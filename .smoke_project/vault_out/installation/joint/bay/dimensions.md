## Overview

A **joint bay** is a controlled excavation or chamber where [[installation.jointing]] of high-voltage cables is performed. Its dimensions must accommodate the joint itself, working space for personnel, and any prefabricated joint components. The minimum recommended dimensions for a joint bay are **3 m × 1.5 m** (length × width).^[8913d160a82c281a:file] This ensures sufficient room for [[installation.prefabricated.joints]] and for maintaining a clean, dry environment during the jointing process.

## Requirements

- **Minimum length**: 3 m
- **Minimum width**: 1.5 m
- The bay must be kept free of standing water and debris to meet the controlled‑environment requirement for jointing.^[8913d160a82c281a:file]
