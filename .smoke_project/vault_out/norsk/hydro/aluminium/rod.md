Norsk Hydro supplies aluminium rod certified to IEC 60228 for use as conductor in high-voltage cable systems [[high-voltage.cable.systems]]. This rod is listed on the Approved Supplier List for HV Cable Components, ensuring its suitability for power transmission applications. The certification guarantees that the rod meets international standards for electrical conductivity and dimensional tolerances. ^[66fee6e56f742178]
