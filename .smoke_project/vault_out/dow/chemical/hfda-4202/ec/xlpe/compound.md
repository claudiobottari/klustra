## Overview

Dow Chemical HFDA-4202 EC is an extra-clean, peroxide-crosslinkable XLPE (cross-linked polyethylene) compound used as insulation material in high-voltage cable systems. It is listed on the approved supplier list for HV cable components, alongside Borealis Visico LE4253, as a preferred insulation compound.^[66fee6e56f742178]

## Properties and Application

As an XLPE compound, HFDA-4202 EC is designed for the [[triple.extrusion.process]] and subsequent [[peroxide.crosslinking]] to form a durable insulation layer. The "extra-clean" designation indicates a low level of contaminants, which is critical for suppressing [[water.treeing]] and [[electrical.treeing]] in high-voltage cables. The compound includes an [[antioxidant.package]] to enhance long-term thermal stability.

## Approval and Standards

The compound is approved for use in high-voltage cable systems, meeting the requirements of relevant IEC standards for insulation materials. It is sourced from Dow Chemical and is part of the approved supplier list for HV cable components.^[66fee6e56f742178]
