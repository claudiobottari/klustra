Aurubis Copper Rod is an oxygen-free copper rod manufactured by Aurubis AG, certified to EN 13601. It is approved for use as a conductor material in high-voltage cable systems, as listed on the approved supplier list for HV cable components.^[66fee6e56f742178] The rod is specifically designated for conductor applications, alongside Norsk Hydro's aluminium rod, which is certified to IEC 60228.^[66fee6e56f742178]
