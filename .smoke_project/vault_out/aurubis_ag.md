## Overview

Aurubis AG is an approved supplier of copper rod for high-voltage cable conductors. The copper rod is oxygen-free and certified to EN 13601, making it suitable for use in [[high_voltage_cable_systems]] where high conductivity and reliability are required.^[f55e45b580b53352:1]

## Product specifications

- **Material**: Oxygen-free copper rod
- **Certification**: EN 13601
- **Application**: Conductor in HV cables

## Role in the supply chain

Aurubis AG is listed alongside [[norsk_hydro]] (aluminium rod, IEC 60228) as a primary conductor supplier. Other approved suppliers for insulation compounds include [[borealis_visico_le4253]] and [[dow_chemical_hfda_4202_ec]]; for sheathing, [[exxonmobil_hdpe_compound]] and [[arkema_aluminium_pe_laminate_tape]]; and for accessories, [[prysmian_pre_moulded_joints_and_terminations]] and [[nkt_cold_shrink_joints]].^[f55e45b580b53352:1]
