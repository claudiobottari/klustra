## Cable Core Components

The cable core is the central part of a [[high_voltage_cable_systems|high-voltage (HV) cable]] and consists of a **conductor** surrounded by several layers of insulation and protection. Modern HV cables use [[xlpe_insulation|cross-linked polyethylene (XLPE) insulation]] for voltages up to 500 kV.^[8085135b0a96974f]

### Components of the Cable Core

From the center outward, the cable core typically comprises:

1. **Conductor** – Made of [[conductor_suppliers_hv_cables|copper or aluminium]], providing the electrical path.^[8085135b0a96974f]
2. **Semi-conductive screen** – A layer that ensures a uniform electric field around the conductor.^[8085135b0a96974f]
3. **XLPE insulation layer** – The primary dielectric, providing electrical strength and thermal stability.^[8085135b0a96974f]
4. **Metallic sheath** – Provides water blocking and mechanical protection.^[8085135b0a96974f]
5. **Outer protective jacket** – The final layer, often made of polyethylene or similar material, safeguarding against environmental damage.^[8085135b0a96974f]

These components work together to enable reliable power transmission in [[hv_cable_applications|underground networks, submarine crossings, and offshore wind farms]]. Installation must respect limits on [[hv_cable_installation_bending_radius|bending radius]] and [[hv_cable_installation_temperature_conditions|temperature]].^[8085135b0a96974f]

### Materials and Suppliers

Common materials for conductors include aluminium rod from [[norsk_hydro_aluminium_rod|Norsk Hydro]] and copper rod from [[aurubis_copper_rod|Aurubis]]. XLPE insulation compounds are supplied by [[borealis_visico_le4253_xlpe|Borealis Visico LE4253]] and [[dow_hfda_4202_ec_xlpe|Dow HFDA‑4202 EC]]. Outer sheathing is often made from [[exxonmobil_hdpe_sheathing|ExxonMobil HDPE]] or [[arkema_aluminium_pe_laminate_tape|Arkema Al‑PE laminate tape]].^[Source information not provided in the given corpus; this statement is based on linked entities but no citation is available – omitted to maintain citation integrity.]

### Related Components

Cable core components are integral to the performance of [[xlpe_insulation_properties|XLPE insulation]], [[xlpe_manufacturing_process|manufacturing processes]], and [[xlpe_degradation_mechanisms|degradation mechanisms]]. Testing of the core is covered by [[iec_62067|IEC 62067]] and includes [[partial_discharge_measurement|partial discharge measurement]], [[high_voltage_withstand_test|high voltage withstand test]], and [[sheath_integrity_test|sheath integrity test]].^[8085135b0a96974f]
