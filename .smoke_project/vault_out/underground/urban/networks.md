Underground urban networks are a primary application of [[high-voltage.cable.systems]] systems. These systems typically use [[xlpe.insulation]] cables with [[copper.conductor]] or [[aluminium.conductor]], [[semi-conductive.screen]], [[metallic.sheath]], and [[outer.protective.jacket]] layers^[8085135b0a96974f:High-Voltage Cable Systems]. Installation of cables in urban underground networks requires careful handling of [[bending.radii]] and thermal considerations^[8085135b0a96974f:High-Voltage Cable Systems].
