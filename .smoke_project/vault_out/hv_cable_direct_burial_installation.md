direct burial installation of [[high_voltage_cable_systems]] is a method where cables are placed directly in excavated trenches and covered with backfill. the requirements for such installations are specified in standard installation guides.

## general requirements

the following general requirements apply to all hv cable installations, including direct burial:
- minimum bending radius during installation: 20× cable outer diameter ^[8913d160a82c281a]
- maximum pulling tension: 50 N/mm² on the conductor ^[8913d160a82c281a]
- installation temperature: above 0 °C ^[8913d160a82c281a]

## trench and bedding

- trench depth: minimum 800 mm in urban areas, 1200 mm in rural areas ^[8913d160a82c281a]
- a sand bedding layer of 100 mm is placed below and above the cable ^[8913d160a82c281a]

## warning tape and backfill

- a warning tape is placed 300 mm above the cable ^[8913d160a82c281a]
- backfill with compacted excavated material ^[8913d160a82c281a]
