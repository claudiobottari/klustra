## Overview

Norsk Hydro is an approved supplier of aluminium rod for use as conductor material in [[high_voltage_cable_systems]]. The rod is certified to IEC 60228, which specifies the requirements for conductors in power cables.^[f55e45b580b53352]

## Products

Norsk Hydro supplies aluminium rod that meets the conductor standards for high-voltage cable cores. The rod is intended for use in [[cable_core]] construction, often as part of [[cross_linked_polyethylene_insulation]] cable systems.^[f55e45b580b53352]

## Certifications

The aluminium rod from Norsk Hydro is certified to IEC 60228, ensuring compliance with international conductor standards for cable applications.^[f55e45b580b53352]
