## Regulatory Framework

All HV cable work must comply with [[IEC 62067]] and [[IEC 61936-1]] (Power installations exceeding 1 kV), local electrical safety regulations, and company-specific safety procedures.^[46d617e505500254:1]

## Safe Working Practices

Before any work on cable systems:
1. Isolate and earth the cable at both ends.
2. Verify absence of voltage using approved indicators.
3. Apply safety locks and tags.
4. Establish a safe work zone with barriers.^[46d617e505500254:2]

These practices are part of the broader [[hv_cable_safety_standards]] and are enforced under the [[hv_cable_regulatory_framework]].

## Emergency Procedures

In case of a cable fault during live operation:
- Maintain an exclusion zone of 3 m around the suspected fault location.
- Do not attempt to re-energize without inspection.
- Report immediately to the control centre.^[46d617e505500254:3]

For further emergency response details, see [[hv_cable_emergency_procedures]].

## Related Standards and Practices
