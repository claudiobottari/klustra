## Regulatory Framework

All high-voltage cable work must comply with [[iec_61936_1]] (Power installations exceeding 1 kV), [[local_electrical_safety_regulations]], and [[company_specific_safety_procedures]].^[b359cf61b264db52:1]

## Safe Working Practices

Before any work on cable systems, the following steps are mandatory:
1. Isolate and earth the cable at both ends ([[cable_isolation_and_earthing]]).
2. Verify absence of voltage using approved indicators ([[voltage_absence_verification]]).
3. Apply safety locks and tags ([[safety_locks_and_tags]]).
4. Establish a safe work zone with barriers ([[safe_work_zone_with_barriers]]).^[b359cf61b264db52:2]

## Emergency Procedures

In case of a cable fault during live operation:
- Maintain an exclusion zone of 3 m around the suspected fault location ([[fault_exclusion_zone]]).
- Do not attempt to re-energize without inspection.
- Report immediately to the control centre.^[b359cf61b264db52:3]
