## Dow HFDA-4202 EC XLPE

'''Dow HFDA-4202 EC''' is an extra-clean [[xlpe_insulation|XLPE (cross-linked polyethylene)]] compound manufactured by [[dow_hfda_4202_ec_xlpe|Dow Chemical]] for use as [[insulation_compound_suppliers_hv_cables|insulation in high-voltage cables]]. It is listed on the [[approved_supplier_list_hv_cable_components|approved supplier list]] for insulation compounds in [[high_voltage_cable_systems|high-voltage cable systems]] alongside alternatives such as [[borealis_visico_le4253_xlpe|Borealis Visico LE4253]].^[66fee6e56f742178:1]

The designation "EC" indicates the compound is of extra-clean quality, a critical requirement for [[xlpe_compound_quality|XLPE compound quality]] in [[hv_cable_applications|HV cable applications]] where contaminants can initiate [[xlpe_degradation_mechanisms|degradation mechanisms]] and reduce service life. The compound is peroxide-crosslinkable and supplied in pellet form for use in the [[xlpe_manufacturing_process|XLPE manufacturing process]] for cable cores.
