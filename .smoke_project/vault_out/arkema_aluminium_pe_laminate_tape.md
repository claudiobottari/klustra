## Overview

Arkema aluminium-PE laminate tape is a sheathing component approved for high-voltage cable systems. It consists of a laminate of aluminium and polyethylene (PE), designed to serve as a radial moisture barrier within the cable's outer protective jacket. The tape is typically applied over the metallic sheath and under the outer jacket, providing an additional layer of protection against water ingress, which is critical for maintaining the integrity of cross-linked polyethylene (XLPE) insulation and preventing water treeing.

## Approved Supplier Status

Arkema is listed as an approved supplier of aluminium-PE laminate tape for cable sheathing in high-voltage cable systems. The tape is used in conjunction with HDPE compounds from ExxonMobil, which are also approved for sheathing applications. This approval is documented in the supplier list for HV cable components, which includes materials for conductor, insulation, sheathing, and accessories.^[f55e45b580b53352]

## Application in Cable Construction

In a typical high-voltage cable construction, the cable core (conductor with XLPE insulation and semi-conductive screens) is enclosed by a metallic sheath, followed by the aluminium-PE laminate tape, and finally an outer protective jacket. The tape acts as a radial moisture barrier, preventing water from penetrating the cable and reaching the insulation. This is especially important for cables installed in wet environments, such as direct burial, duct installations, or submarine crossings, where moisture ingress can lead to water treeing and premature failure of the XLPE insulation.

## Related Components and Standards

The aluminium-PE laminate tape is part of a broader set of approved sheathing materials. Other approved sheathing components include ExxonMobil HDPE compounds. The tape is used in cable systems that must comply with relevant standards such as IEC 62067 (power cables with extruded insulation and their accessories for rated voltages above 150 kV up to 500 kV) and IEC 61936-1 (power installations exceeding 1 kV AC). Installation practices for cables using this tape must follow guidelines for minimum bending radius, maximum pulling tension, and installation temperature to avoid damaging the laminate layer.

## Testing and Quality Assurance

Cables incorporating Arkema aluminium-PE laminate tape are subject to type testing, routine testing, and after-installation testing as per applicable standards. Sheath integrity tests, such as DC or VLF withstand testing, are performed to verify that the moisture barrier (including the laminate tape) is intact after installation. Joint resistance measurements and partial discharge measurements may also be conducted to ensure overall cable system reliability.
