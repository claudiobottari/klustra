## Scope

IEC 61936‑1 applies to power installations operating at voltages above 1 kV a.c. It establishes minimum safety requirements for the design, erection, operation, and maintenance of such installations, including high‑voltage cable systems. The standard is intended to protect persons, livestock, and property from electrical hazards. ^[b359cf61b264db52:Safety Standards for HV Cable Work]

## Regulatory Framework

All work on high‑voltage cable systems must comply with:

- **IEC 61936‑1** (the primary international standard) ^[b359cf61b264db52:Safety Standards for HV Cable Work]
- [[local_electrical_safety_regulations]] – national or regional codes that may impose additional or more stringent requirements ^[b359cf61b264db52:Safety Standards for HV Cable Work]
- [[company_specific_safety_procedures]] – internal rules that reflect the specific risks of the installation and the operator’s experience ^[b359cf61b264db52:Safety Standards for HV Cable Work]

## Safe Working Practices

Before any work on cable systems, the following steps must be taken in accordance with IEC 61936‑1:

1. **Isolate and earth** the cable at both ends ([[cable_isolation_and_earthing]]). ^[b359cf61b264db52:Safety Standards for HV Cable Work]
2. **Verify absence of voltage** using approved voltage indicators ([[voltage_absence_verification]]). ^[b359cf61b264db52:Safety Standards for HV Cable Work]
3. **Apply safety locks and tags** to prevent inadvertent re‑energisation ([[safety_locks_and_tags]]). ^[b359cf61b264db52:Safety Standards for HV Cable Work]
4. **Establish a safe work zone** with barriers to restrict access ([[safe_work_zone_with_barriers]]). ^[b359cf61b264db52:Safety Standards for HV Cable Work]

## Emergency Procedures

In the event of a cable fault during live operation, IEC 61936‑1 requires:

- Maintain an exclusion zone of at least 3 m around the suspected fault location ([[fault_exclusion_zone]]). ^[b359cf61b264db52:Safety Standards for HV Cable Work]
- Do **not** attempt to re‑energise the circuit without a thorough inspection. ^[b359cf61b264db52:Safety Standards for HV Cable Work]
- Report the incident immediately to the control centre ([[cable_fault_emergency_procedure]]). ^[b359cf61b264db52:Safety Standards for HV Cable Work]
