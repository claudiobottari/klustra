The reliability of high-voltage (HV) cable systems depends critically on the quality of their sheathing, which provides radial moisture barriers and mechanical protection. This cluster gathers all members related to the approval and sourcing of sheathing materials for HV cables. The central resource is the [[approved_supplier_list_hv_cable_components]], which curates qualified suppliers across all cable components, including conductors, insulation, and accessories. Within that list, the [[sheathing_suppliers_hv_cables]] page specifically focuses on approved vendors for sheathing materials, such as HDPE compounds and aluminium-polyethylene laminate tapes.

Two key suppliers are highlighted in this cluster. [[ExxonMobil HDPE sheathing]] supplies a high-density polyethylene compound approved for use as a radial moisture barrier in HV cable construction. Similarly, [[Arkema aluminium-PE laminate tape]] provides an aluminium-polyethylene laminate tape that serves as an effective radial moisture barrier. Both materials are essential for ensuring the long-term durability and electrical performance of HV cables, and their inclusion in the approved supplier list underscores the rigorous standards required for these critical components.

By grouping these pages together, the cluster provides a comprehensive view of sheathing material sourcing—from the overarching list of approved vendors down to the specific products offered by leading suppliers. This structure supports engineers and procurement specialists in quickly identifying qualified sheathing options and understanding the technical specifications that make them suitable for high-voltage applications.
