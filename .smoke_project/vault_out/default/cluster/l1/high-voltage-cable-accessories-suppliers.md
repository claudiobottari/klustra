This cluster brings together information on approved suppliers and specific product types for high-voltage (HV) cable accessories. The central theme is the selection and specification of reliable components—such as joints and terminations—that are critical for the safe and efficient operation of HV cable systems. The cluster is anchored by the page [[accessory_suppliers_hv_cables]], which lists approved suppliers like Prysmian and NKT and provides context on testing, installation, and relevant standards such as IEC 62067.

Two key product lines from these approved suppliers are detailed in the member pages. [[prysmian_pre_moulded_joints_terminations]] describes factory-moulded, IEC type-tested joints and terminations for high-voltage XLPE cables, emphasizing their pre-moulded construction and compliance with industry standards. [[nkt_cold_shrink_joints]] focuses on NKT's cold-shrink joints for the 66–170 kV range, highlighting their installation method and their inclusion on the approved supplier list.

Together, these pages provide a comprehensive view of the HV cable accessories landscape, from the overarching supplier approval framework to the specific technical characteristics of major product offerings. This structure supports engineers and procurement specialists in making informed decisions about component selection for high-voltage cable systems.
