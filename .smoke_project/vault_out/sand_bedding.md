## Sand Bedding

**Sand bedding** is a layer of granular material, typically sand, placed around a [[direct_burial|direct-buried]] cable to provide mechanical protection, improve thermal dissipation, and ensure uniform support. According to standard installation practices, a sand bedding layer of **100 mm** is required both below and above the cable.^[4548a58ab1ab402c:Cable Installation Guide]

The bedding is part of a broader trench construction sequence that includes excavation to a specified [[trench_depth]], placement of the bedding, installation of the cable, a covering layer of sand, a [[warning_tape]], and finally [[backfill]] with compacted excavated material.^[4548a58ab1ab402c:Cable Installation Guide]
