## Overview

NKT cold-shrink joints for the 66–170 kV voltage range are listed as approved accessories for [[high-voltage.cable.systems]] under the approved supplier list for HV cable components^[66fee6e56f742178].

## Usage

These joints are intended for use in [[installation.jointing]] of high-voltage cables, particularly in applications such as [[underground.urban.networks]], [[submarine.crossings]], and [[offshore.wind.farm.connections]]^[66fee6e56f742178].
