## Outer Protective Jacket

The **outer protective jacket** is the outermost layer of a [[high_voltage_cable_systems|cable]] assembly, providing mechanical protection and environmental sealing. It is applied over the [[metallic_sheath]] and is a critical component for ensuring long-term reliability in demanding installations.

### Composition and Materials

Typical materials include high-density polyethylene (HDPE) compounds, such as those from [[exxonmobil_hdpe_compound|ExxonMobil]], and cross-linked polyethylene (XLPE) formulations like [[borealis_visico_le4253|Borealis Visico LE4253]].^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1] The jacket may also incorporate a laminated tape, e.g., [[arkema_aluminium_pe_laminate_tape|Arkema aluminium-PE laminate tape]], for enhanced water blocking.^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1]

### Functions

- **Mechanical protection**: Shields the [[cable_core]] and [[metallic_sheath]] from abrasion, impact, and crushing during handling and installation.^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1]
- **Environmental sealing**: Prevents moisture ingress, which can degrade [[xlpe_insulation_material|XLPE insulation]] and cause [[water_treeing]].^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1]
- **Chemical resistance**: Protects against soil chemicals, oils, and other contaminants in [[direct_burial]] or [[duct_installation]] environments.^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1]

### Installation Considerations

During installation, the jacket must withstand the [[maximum_pulling_tension]] without tearing or excessive elongation.^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1] The [[minimum_bending_radius]] of the cable is often limited by the jacket's flexibility; exceeding this can cause cracking or delamination.^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1] In [[duct_installation]], a [[cable_lubricant]] is used to reduce friction and prevent jacket damage.^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1]

### Testing and Quality Assurance

After installation, the jacket's integrity is verified via [[sheath_integrity_test|sheath integrity testing]] (e.g., DC or VLF withstand).^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1] This ensures no punctures or cuts occurred during pulling. [[routine_testing]] of the jacket material includes checks for thickness, hardness, and dielectric strength.^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1]

### Applications

The outer protective jacket is essential in all HV cable applications, including [[underground_urban_networks]], [[submarine_crossings]], and [[offshore_wind_farms]].^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1] It is particularly critical in [[direct_burial]] installations, where the jacket is in direct contact with the [[backfill]] material.^[acd06c178fe5b8e6:1]
