# Prysmian Pre-moulded Joints and Terminations

**Prysmian** is an approved supplier of pre‑moulded joints and terminations for high‑voltage cable systems. These accessories are factory‑manufactured to simplify field installation and reduce the risk of assembly errors. Prysmian’s products are IEC type‑tested, confirming compliance with international standards for electrical performance and reliability.^[66fee6e56f742178:1]

## Applications
Pre‑moulded joints and terminations are typically used with [[xlpe.insulation]] cables in [[high‑voltage.cable.systems]] for [[underground.urban.networks]], [[submarine.crossings]], and [[offshore.wind.farm.connections]].

## Specifications
The approved supplier list notes that Prysmian’s pre‑moulded joints and terminations are “IEC type‑tested”, indicating they meet the requirements of relevant IEC standards such as [[iec.62067]] for power cables and their accessories.^[66fee6e56f742178:1]
