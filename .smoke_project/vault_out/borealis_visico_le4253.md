## Visico LE4253

**Visico LE4253** is a [[peroxide_crosslinking|peroxide-crosslinkable]] [[cross_linked_polyethylene_insulation|cross‑linked polyethylene (XLPE)]] insulation compound supplied by Borealis. It is listed as an approved insulation compound for [[high_voltage_cable_systems|high‑voltage cable components]] in the supplier list of an unspecified manufacturer or project.^[f55e45b580b53352]

### Properties and classification

The compound is categorised as a [[xlpe_insulation_material|XLPE insulation material]] and is designed for peroxide crosslinking – a process that transforms the polyethylene into a thermoset network, giving the insulation its characteristic high [[dielectric_strength|dielectric strength]] and [[operating_temperature|operating temperature]] capability. While the source does not provide numerical property data, the material is specified to be used in the insulation layer of high‑voltage cables.^[f55e45b580b53352]
