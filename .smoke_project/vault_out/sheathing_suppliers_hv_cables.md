## Sheathing Suppliers for HV Cables

Sheathing materials for [[high_voltage_cable_systems]] must provide robust mechanical protection and, in many designs, a moisture barrier to prevent water treeing in [[xlpe_insulation]]. The approved supplier list includes two primary sources:

### HDPE Sheathing Compound
- **ExxonMobil** supplies a high-density polyethylene (HDPE) compound formulated specifically for radial moisture barrier applications. This compound is used as the outer sheath in multilayer [[cable_core_components]] designs.^[66fee6e56f742178:0]

### Moisture Barrier Tape
- **Arkema** provides an aluminium-polyethylene laminate tape, which is applied as a longitudinal moisture barrier beneath the outer sheath or as part of the sheath construction.^[66fee6e56f742178:0]

These components are integral to [[hv_cable_applications]] where long-term reliability in wet environments is critical. For the complete list of approved suppliers for all cable components, see [[approved_supplier_list_hv_cable_components]].
