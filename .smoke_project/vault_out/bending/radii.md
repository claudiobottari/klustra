In high-voltage cable systems, careful handling of bending radii is required during installation to maintain the integrity of the cable^[8085135b0a96974f:High-Voltage Cable Systems]. This is particularly important for cables with [[xlpe.insulation|XLPE insulation]] and [[copper.conductor|copper]] or [[aluminium.conductor|aluminium]] conductors, which are deployed in applications such as [[underground.urban.networks]], [[submarine.crossings]], and [[offshore.wind.farm.connections]]. The bending radius directly affects the performance of components like the [[semi-conductive.screen]], [[metallic.sheath]], and [[outer.protective.jacket]], and must be considered during installation to avoid damage.
