## Conductor suppliers for HV cables

Approved suppliers for conductor materials in high-voltage cable systems are specified in the [[approved_supplier_list_hv_cable_components]]. For aluminium conductors, the sole approved rod supplier is [[norsk_hydro_aluminium_rod|Norsk Hydro]], whose aluminium rod is certified to IEC 60228.^[66fee6e56f742178:1] For copper conductors, the approved supplier is [[aurubis_copper_rod|Aurubis AG]], which supplies oxygen-free copper rod certified to EN 13601.^[66fee6e56f742178:1] These materials are used in the manufacture of [[cable_core_components]] for [[high_voltage_cable_systems]].
