Aurubis AG supplies oxygen-free copper rod used as a conductor material in high-voltage cable systems. The product is certified to EN 13601, ensuring compliance with industry standards for electrical conductivity and purity. As a key component in [[copper.conductor]] manufacturing, Aurubis copper rod is listed on the approved supplier list for HV cable components, alongside [[norsk.hydro.aluminium.rod]] for aluminium alternatives. The certification to EN 13601 guarantees the material meets stringent requirements for oxygen-free copper, which is critical for minimizing electrical losses and ensuring long-term reliability in power transmission applications.^[66fee6e56f742178]
